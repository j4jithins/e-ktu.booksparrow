AIR QUALITY MANAGEMENT



Syllabus

Air pollution-sources, effects on human, vegetation, environment, air pollutants. Indoor pollution. Meteorology, factors affecting dispersion of pollutants, Plume behaviour. Modelling of air pollutants, Dispersion modelling. Monitoring of pollutants-Particulate and gaseous, Control of air pollutants-Methods for particulate and gaseous pollutants, Air quality legislations


  Module I
15%
Introduction- Components of Environment- Definition -Air Pollution- History of air pollution episodes-Various Sources of Air pollution - Air Pollutants- Types of Air Pollutants

  Module II
15%
Effect of air pollutants on health, vegetation, animals and materials and environment, Green house effect - Indoor Air Pollution, sources of indoor air pollutants
FIRST INTERNAL EXAMINATION

  Module III
15%
Meteorological aspects of Air Pollutant Dispersion - Temperature and Pressure relationships-Atmospheric Stability- Temperature Lapse Rate- Inversions- Types, Plume behavior

  Module IV
15%
Dispersion of Air pollutants-Plume dispersion theory- Gaussian plume model (Derivation not required)- Assumptions-Advantages and Disadvantages- Pasquill's stability curves , Dispersion problems involving point source and line source - Estimation of plume rise.
SECOND INTERNAL EXAMINATION

  Module V
20%
Air Quality monitoring - Ambient air sampling - Collection of gaseous air pollutants-Collection of particulate Pollutants- Ambient Air Quality standards

  Module VI
20%
Control of Air Pollutants- Particulate emission control-methods, Scrubbing-Cyclones- Filtration- Electrostatic Precipitation-Gaseous emission control- adsorption, absorption, thermal methods
END SEMESTER EXAM


Course Objectives
1. To understand the various forms of air pollutants and their effects on human and environment
2. To know the various methods of controlling air pollutants

Expected Outcome
  1. Create an awareness among students regarding air pollution problems
  2. To understand the various techniques that can be adopted for managing air pollution related problems.
