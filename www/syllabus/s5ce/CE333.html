GEOTECHNICAL ENGINEERING LAB



Prerequisite: : CE208 Geotechnical Engineering - I



list of Exercises :
  1. Determination of Water Content, Specific Gravity and Shrinkage Limit
  2. Field Density determination and Sieve Analysis
  3. Atterberg Limits (Liquid Limit and Plastic Limit)
  4. Hydrometer Analysis
  5. Direct Shear test
  6. Standard Proctor Compaction Test
  7. Permeability Test and Unconfined Compression Test
  8. Consolidation Test
  9. Swelling Test
  10. Heavy compaction
  11. California Bearing Ratio Test

ï‚· To understand the laboratory tests used for determination of physical, index and Engineering properties of soil


Expected Outcomes
The students will
  • have thorough knowledge about the procedures of laboratory tests used for determination of physical, index and engineering properties of soils
  • have the capability to classify soils based on test results and interpret engineering behavior based on test results
  • be able to evaluate the permeability and shear strength of soils
  • be able to evaluate settlement characteristics of soils
  • be able to evaluate compaction characteristics required for field application
