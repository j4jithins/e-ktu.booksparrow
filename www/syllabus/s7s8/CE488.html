Course Objectives
  1. To provide an overview of the common hazards and their dynamics
  2. To inculcate the basic concepts of disaster management


Expected Outcome
The students will be able to:
  1. get general ideas about the processes involved in natural and anthropogenic disasters
  2. understand the concepts of disaster management and measures to mitigate and contain common episodes of disasters
