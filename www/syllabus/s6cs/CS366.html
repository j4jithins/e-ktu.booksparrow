Course Objectives
  1. To introduce the fundamentals of Language processing from the algorithmic viewpoint.
  2. To discuss various issues those make natural language processing a hard task.
  3. To discuss some applications of Natural Language Processing (NLP).


Expected Outcome
  1. appreciate the fundamental concepts of Natural Language Processing.
    2. 2
  2. design algorithms for NLP tasks.
  3. develop useful systems for language processing and related tasks involving text processing.
